about our company

MPIYAKHE 04 CONSTRUCTION is a developing company based in the Nelson Mandela Bay Metro and specialising in civil work and building construction and traffic services. The core and backbone of our company is made up with management and interests of a well known and respected local business. We are CIDB Grade 7CE, 7GB, 1SQ, 1EP with proven capabilities over a wide range of different fields in the Civil and building industry. MPIYAKHE 04 CONSTRUCTION is a Level 1 Contributor with 100% black owned.
